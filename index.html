Creating Variables

Description

Welcome to the Creating Variables Exercise! This exercise will give you practice using "const" and "let" to instantiate variables.

Instructions

  1. Open up index.js in your editor.
  2. Follow the instructions in the comment at the top of that file.
  3. Refresh this page after saving changes to see your progress.
